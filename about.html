Blockchain

Blockchains are used to keep records of all the tradeing of cryptocurrencies. All records involving the coins are recorded as blocks. After the blocks are recorded it is impossible to alter any of the data on the block. This is one reason why all the major cryptocurrencies are using blockchains to keep their records.
